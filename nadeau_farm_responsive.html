Our story

Rooted in this land
for over 85 years.

Nadeau Farm has been in our family since 1939. Today it's run by Leah and her husband — just the two of them — giving rescued livestock a safe and loving home in the heart of Hamden.

Every egg we sell and every yoga class we host goes right back to feeding and caring for the animals. It's a simple mission, and one we're proud to keep alive.
