Our story

Family farming,
rooted in rescue.

Nadeau Farm has been in our family since 1939. Today it's run by Leah and her husband — just the two of them — giving rescued livestock a safe, loving home. Every egg sold, every yoga class held, goes right back to caring for the animals.
